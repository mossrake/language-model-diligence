2. Description of Service

The Language Model Diligence Self-Assessment is a free, self-service questionnaire that evaluates the coverage posture of a single AI model deployed in a defined process. The assessment consists of five questions that produce a tier designation (Calibrated, Partial, or Uncertain) based on the relationship between the stated consequence level and the respondent's self-reported coverage across four dimensions: Identity, Drift, Verification, and Recovery.

The self-service evaluation produces a Self-Assessment – a self-certified result based entirely on information provided by you. The Self-Assessment includes a tier designation, a coverage summary, and a radar visualization of coverage against consequence.

Assessment of processes involving multiple models, pipelines, or agentic architectures is beyond the scope of this self-service instrument and requires a facilitated engagement with Mossrake.
