# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in Language Model Diligence.

## Feedback

This repository does not use GitHub Issues. Bug reports, questions, and feedback on the Self-Assessment instrument and framework materials are welcome by email: info@mossrake.com.

Substantive feedback on the framework itself — question anchors, scoring interpretation, dimension definitions — is particularly valuable and may inform future revisions, with acknowledgment where appropriate. Note that feedback is subject to the Feedback provisions of the Terms of Use.

## Pull Requests

This repository does not accept unsolicited pull requests.

The materials here are published for transparency and reference under the terms in [LICENSE.md](LICENSE.md), not developed as a community project. Maintaining a single, unambiguous chain of ownership over the framework and instrument is necessary to support the licensing paths described in LICENSE.md, including future publication of certain artifacts under different terms.

If you would like to propose a change, describe it by email. If a contribution is invited, it will require a signed contributor agreement assigning or broadly licensing the contribution to Mossrake Group, LLC before it can be merged.

## Machine-Readable Specifications and Tooling

Schemas, conformance fixtures, and reference tooling for framework-conformant integrations are under development and will be published in separate repositories under their own license terms, with their own contribution policies.
